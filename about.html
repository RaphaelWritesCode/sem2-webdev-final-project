Tid Bit About Me

 Hi, my name is Raphael Antioquia, a proud native of Scarborough, with both of my parents hailing from the Philippines. Although I'm currently a registered nurse, I'm on leave to chase a new career path. As a mature student, my hobbies span from tennis and working out to playing video games and watching anime.

 This semester, Overwatch became my obsession, partly because a wrist injury prevented me from playing tennis. Since its beta phase, I've become quite familiar with the game, even reaching a peak rank of Masters. My commitment to improving my gameplay and the cherished memories of playing with friends online have turned Overwatch into more than just a game for me—it was a passion and was a fond part of my daily life. That's why I would like to share it with you! I also, didn't realize I could've chose a non-video game option, aha. I was in too deep however.
